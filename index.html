About Us

SuperHeroes In Training

Superheroes in Training is a website for my final project in Prog2700 at NSCC. The concept is a site that sells pieces of artwork, but not just any artwork! Artwork of the heroes of tomorrow made by the young minds of today!

The Tool Of The Trade

Each piece of art is meticulously pieced together line by line. Rumors whispered among crowds, is that each of the pencils used in every drawing is handcrafted from the branches of The Great Basin Bristlecone Pine.

Inspiration Of Life By Color

The color palettes of each piece are inspired by the colors of Thomdril "Thom" Merrilin's cloak, gleeing with colors off every stroke. Colors used are said to be so coalesced they have become naked to the human eye. Example above.

Quest For Revelation

Our goal, bring revelation through art. Speak to the soul through the eyes. Feeding a thought with an inspiration. Explore the unexplored human expression. If the pen is mightier than the sword, then this page is our Valhalla.

Corruption Man

Banned in 190 countries, by everyone from Hans Ulrich Obrist to Okwui Enwezor. Comes the masterpiece that is Corruption Man. Brendon Currie found a box of canvases when he was eight years old while working as a retreat pillar miner in Tampakan Mines, located in the tri-boundaries of Davao del Sur, Sarangani, and South Cotobato. He swore to paint only as long as he had original canvas left.

Ninja Team

6-year-old Axel made a bet with a pianist in a Bordello somewhere in Eastern Europe that, with three colors, he could draw a masterpiece. The period was the 800s, tailing the end of the middle ages. The artwork "Ninja Team" can be traced back as one of the original art pieces that inspired the modern-day superhero dating back to Lee Falk, author, and creator of "Mandrake The Magician" and "The Phantom."

Super Mila

In the art world, Mila, called The Mother, uses the same nine brushes called "The Muses" named Calliope, Clio, Erato, Euterpe, Melpomene, Polyhymnia Terpsichore, Thalia, and Urania. Her imagery has been said to inspire modern-day poetry, lyrics, and myths. Great collectors have said that Mila is a descendant of Myron of Eleutherae whose lost works have been rebirthed through the strokes of "The Muses."
